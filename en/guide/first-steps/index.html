📖 How TachoMobile 2 Works

TachoMobile 2 is an intelligent driver assistant that helps monitor driving time, breaks, rest periods and compliance with EU driving regulations.

The application works similarly to a digital tachograph by automatically recognizing driver activities using GPS data and maintaining an electronic record of the working day.

🚀 1. Starting Your Working Day

Grant all required permissions and verify that everything is correctly configured in the Application Status screen.

Then select Start Work / Insert Driver Card. From this point on, TachoMobile runs automatically in the background.

💡 Tip

It is recommended to keep your phone connected to a charger while working, as continuous GPS usage may increase battery consumption.

🚚 2. Automatic Driving Detection

TachoMobile automatically detects when driving starts and ends, updating driver activities without requiring manual input.

The application analyzes GPS data together with device behavior to recognize driving activity as accurately as possible.

☕ 3. Breaks

During longer breaks it is recommended to disable automatic driving detection.

This helps prevent accidental activity entries caused by walking, moving outside the vehicle or temporary GPS signal loss.

However, this is not mandatory. TachoMobile includes intelligent algorithms designed to minimize false driving detections.

✏️ 4. Editing Activities

Every activity can be edited, deleted or changed at any time.

If an activity has been detected incorrectly, it can easily be corrected manually.

🏁 5. Finishing Your Working Day

At the end of your shift select Remove Driver Card / Finish Work.

The application will stop GPS monitoring to reduce unnecessary battery usage.

📊 6. Analysis & Premium Features

Based on collected data, TachoMobile automatically analyzes:

  • Driving time
  • Breaks
  • Daily rest periods
  • Weekly rest periods
  • Driving limits
  • Potential infringements
  • Working time statistics

The Free version uses exactly the same calculation engine as the Premium version. Automatic driving detection and core monitoring features work identically in both versions.

Premium and the Free Trial unlock all advanced features, including detailed analysis, complete history, advanced statistics, reports, configurable alerts and notifications before driving limits or possible infringements are reached.

✅ That's It!

Once configured, almost everything works automatically.

In everyday use, the driver only needs to:

  1. Start the working day.
  2. Finish work at the end of the shift.

TachoMobile 2 takes care of everything else.

⚠️ Important Information

TachoMobile 2 is a driver assistance tool. It does not replace a digital tachograph or any recording equipment required by applicable laws and regulations.

The application uses GPS data together with proprietary activity recognition algorithms. Although continuously improved, occasional incorrect activity detection may occur due to GPS limitations, Android system restrictions or specific device characteristics.

Drivers should always verify recorded activities. In the event of any discrepancy, the official tachograph records and applicable legislation always take precedence.
