```html TachoMobile 2 - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TachoMobile 2 ("the App") collects, uses, stores, processes, and protects user information.

1. General Information

TachoMobile 2 is a driver assistant application designed to help users monitor driving time, work activities, breaks, availability periods, and other transport-related information.

The App is intended for professional and personal use related to driver activity tracking and time management.

2. Information Collected

Depending on the features used, the App may collect and process:

  • Location data, including background location access, used for automatic activity detection and optionally stored as part of user activity records;
  • Motion and activity recognition data;
  • Driver activity records and user-generated operational data entered or generated within the App;
  • Cloud synchronization metadata required to provide backup and restore functionality;
  • User account identifiers used for authentication and synchronization through Firebase Authentication;
  • Application diagnostics, crash reports, and technical information;
  • Anonymous analytics information;
  • Purchase and subscription status information provided by Google Play Billing.

3. How Information Is Used

Collected information is used solely for:

  • Automatic driving detection;
  • Driving and rest time calculations;
  • Generating driver activity reports;
  • Providing activity timeline functionality;
  • Synchronizing and restoring user data across devices when cloud synchronization is enabled;
  • Providing backup functionality;
  • Improving application stability and reliability;
  • Providing premium subscription functionality;
  • Preventing abuse, crashes, fraud, and technical issues.

The App does not sell personal information to third parties.

4. Local and Cloud Data Storage

Most application data is stored locally on the user's device.

If the user voluntarily enables cloud synchronization and backup functionality, selected application data may also be securely stored using Google Firebase services, including Firebase Firestore.

Cloud-stored data may include:

  • Driver activity records;
  • Synchronization metadata;
  • Backup information;
  • Device synchronization identifiers;
  • Location information contained within activity records;
  • Other information necessary to restore application data and synchronize data across devices.

Cloud synchronization is optional and may be enabled or disabled by the user at any time.

Users remain responsible for securing their devices, exported files, and access to their synchronization accounts.

5. Location Information

TachoMobile 2 may collect location information to support automatic driving detection and activity-related functionality.

Location information is not used for continuous public tracking of users.

When cloud synchronization is enabled, location data that forms part of activity records may be included in synchronized backups solely for backup and restore purposes.

At the time of publication of this Privacy Policy, the App does not provide functionality allowing users to share their real-time location or synchronized activity records with other users.

6. Exported Reports

The App allows users to generate and export PDF reports.

These reports are created locally on the user's device and remain under the user's control.

Exported files may contain user-entered or generated driving activity information.

7. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services including:

  • Google Play Services;
  • Google Play Billing;
  • Firebase Authentication;
  • Firebase Firestore;
  • Firebase Analytics;
  • Firebase Crashlytics.

These providers may process certain technical information according to their own privacy policies and retention practices.

Google services may automatically collect technical identifiers, crash logs, diagnostic information, and anonymous usage statistics necessary for application functionality, security, and performance monitoring.

8. Data Retention

Most user-generated data remains stored locally on the user's device until deleted by the user.

When cloud synchronization is enabled, synchronized data may remain stored in Firebase services until:

  • The user deletes the cloud backup using the App;
  • The user disables synchronization and requests deletion;
  • The data is otherwise removed in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Diagnostic, analytics, and crash-related information processed through Google or Firebase services is retained according to those providers' policies.

9. Data Deletion

Users may permanently delete synchronized cloud data directly from within the App using the cloud backup deletion functionality.

Users may also request deletion of externally processed data by contacting the developer.

Users may remove locally stored data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the application;
  • Clearing application storage in Android settings;
  • Deleting exported files manually;
  • Deleting cloud backups through the App.

10. GDPR / EU Users

Users located within the European Union may have the right to:

  • Request access to their personal data;
  • Request correction of inaccurate information;
  • Request deletion of their personal data;
  • Request restriction of processing;
  • Object to certain types of processing;
  • Request information regarding how their data is processed.

The legal basis for processing may include:

  • User consent;
  • Performance of services explicitly requested by the user;
  • Legitimate interests related to application security, reliability, and fraud prevention;
  • Compliance with legal obligations.

11. Permissions

The App may request permissions including:

  • Location access;
  • Background location access;
  • Internet access;
  • Foreground service access;
  • Notification access;
  • Activity recognition access.

These permissions are required to provide functionality such as automatic driving detection, activity monitoring, synchronization, notifications, report generation, and backup services.

12. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are implemented to help protect user information and prevent unauthorized access.

Data transmitted between the App and cloud services is encrypted in transit.

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

13. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

14. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Changes become effective immediately after publication on this page. Continued use of the App after publication constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

15. Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or data handling practices, you may contact:

tachomobile.support@gmail.com
```
