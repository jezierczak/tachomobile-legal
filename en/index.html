Main Features

Driving Time

Monitor your driving, working time, and breaks in real-time with high precision directly from the main application interface.

Smart Notifications

Stay on track and comply with complex working hours regulations through active and intelligent system alerts.

Driving Analysis

Access detailed work statistics, automated logs, and clear operational reports to keep your tracking organized.

Import .ddd Files

Easily import .ddd files from your driver card or tachograph. The app automatically analyzes historical data, giving you seamless control over your driving time.

Data Management

Securely manage your data logs with easy export options designed for professional and independent transport operations.

About the App

TachoMobile 2 is an Android application designed to assist drivers in monitoring their daily on-road activities.

The project focuses on readability, ease of use, secure cloud synchronization, and user data encryption.

About the Developer

The application is developed by independent programmer Jarosław Jezierczaka. The project uniquely combines practical, long-term experience in the transport industry (including working as a truck driver and machinery operator) with professional mobile software development for the Android platform.
