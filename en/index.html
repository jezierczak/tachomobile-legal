TachoMobile 2

Driver assistant application for monitoring driving activities, work time, breaks, and transport-related information.

About

TachoMobile 2 is an Android application designed to help drivers track driving activity, work periods, breaks, and related transport information.

The application is intended as a helper and organizational tool for professional and private drivers.

Developer

Developed by independent Android developer Jarosław Jezierczak.

Focused on mobile applications, transport-related tools, and long-term Android development.
