About the App

TachoMobile 2 is a modern Android application designed to help professional drivers track driving activity, work periods, availability, and mandatory rest breaks.

It serves as an advanced organizational tool to optimize daily transport routines and maintain personal logs with ease.

Developer

Developed by independent software engineer Jarosław Jezierczak.

The project uniquely combines real-world heavy trucking industry experience with long-term mobile application development to deliver practical, reliable tools for drivers.
