Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how TachoMobile 2 ("the App") collects, uses, stores, and protects user information.

1. General Information

TachoMobile 2 is a driver assistant application designed to help users monitor driving time, work activities, breaks, availability periods, and related transport information.

The App is intended for professional and personal use related to driver activity tracking and time management.

2. Information Collected

Depending on the features used, the App may collect and process:

  • Location data (including background location access)
  • Motion and activity recognition data
  • Application diagnostics and crash information
  • Anonymous analytics information
  • Purchase and subscription status information provided by Google Play Billing

Most operational data generated by the App is stored locally on the user’s device.

3. How Information Is Used

Collected information is used solely for:

  • Automatic driving detection
  • Driving and rest time calculations
  • Generating driver activity reports
  • Providing activity timeline functionality
  • Improving application stability and reliability
  • Providing premium subscription functionality
  • Preventing abuse, crashes, and technical issues

The App does not sell personal information to third parties.

4. Local Data Storage

Most application data is stored locally on the user’s device. TachoMobile 2 does not operate a central cloud platform for storing user driving activity records.

Users are responsible for securing their own device, backups, exported files, and local application data.

5. Exported Reports

The App allows users to generate and export PDF reports. These files are created locally on the device and are fully controlled by the user.

Exported files may contain user-entered or generated driving activity information.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may use third-party services including:

  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Billing
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

These providers may process certain technical information according to their own privacy policies and data retention policies.

Google services may automatically collect technical identifiers, crash logs, diagnostic information, and anonymous usage statistics necessary for application functionality, security, and performance monitoring.

7. Data Retention

TachoMobile 2 stores user data only for as long as necessary to provide application functionality, improve stability, comply with legal obligations, and prevent fraud or abuse.

Most user-generated activity data is stored locally on the user’s device and remains under the user’s direct control.

If diagnostic, analytics, or crash-related information is processed through third-party providers such as Firebase or Google Play Services, retention periods are governed by those providers’ policies.

Users may remove locally stored data at any time by:

  • Uninstalling the application
  • Clearing application storage in Android settings
  • Deleting exported files manually

8. Data Deletion

Users may request deletion of any externally processed data by contacting the developer.

Where applicable, deletion requests will be processed within a reasonable timeframe unless data retention is required for legal, fraud prevention, billing, or security purposes.

Because most data is stored locally on the user’s device, uninstalling the application or clearing application storage generally results in permanent deletion of application data.

9. GDPR / EU Users

Users located in the European Union may have the right to:

  • Request access to their personal data
  • Request deletion of their personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Object to certain types of processing
  • Request restriction of processing

The legal basis for processing data may include user consent, legitimate interest, contractual necessity, and compliance with legal obligations.

10. Permissions

The App may request permissions including:

  • Location access
  • Background location access
  • Internet access
  • Foreground service access
  • Notification access
  • Activity recognition access

These permissions are required for core application functionality such as automatic driving detection, trip tracking, activity monitoring, notifications, and report generation.

11. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to help protect application data and prevent unauthorized access.

However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

12. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13.

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

13. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes become effective immediately after publication on this page.

14. Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy or data handling practices, you may contact:

tachomobile.support@gmail.com
